The African Studies Gallery is a unique exhibition space, focusing on exhibiting and examining African art. The gallery is a not-for-profit organisation that aspires to examine African Art in a modern context.

This exhibition is of urban art, produced by self-taught artists, mainly from Kinshasa. During the past decade both museums and art markets have shown a growing interest in contemporary African art. But what does ‘contemporary African art’ mean in this context?...
